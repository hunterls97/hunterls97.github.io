ENG 2001 - Project Managment

During my second year at York University, I was enrolled in a project management course where I had to participate in developing a project with a team of my colleagues.

The focus on this project was to incorporate good project management techniques to a engineering project of our choosing, without actually executing the project. For our project, we chose to host a theoretical design competition for students around the university. My role in this project was to develop a website to handle advertisements and signup data for the competition.

Our project was well received, and our professor asked us to present it again on a later date in front of a panel of other engineering professors.

McCray Optical Supply Inc. Website

One task I was envolved in when working at McCray Optical Supply was assisting in the development of their website.

I had several roles in the development process from programming the landing page both functionally and from a design perspective, to utilizing DML operations to create plugins and query appropriate data for administration purposes.

The website was built in an ecomerce tool called Prestashop, and utilized many languages such as: PHP, SQL, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. Overall the project was a great experience and served as a good introduction to the web development process.

Street Tree Planting Contract Administration

My first project as a special project developer for the Regional Municipality of York was to develop a contract administration web application for internal business users.

I helped develop three main components of this application: Tree Planting Dashboards and Reports, Tree Warranty Dashboards and Reports, and Contract Preparation Reports. The dashboards in this project consisted of querying data from the database and displaying it in a meaningful and simplified view for the user.
