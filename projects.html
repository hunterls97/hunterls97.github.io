ENG 2001 - Project Managment

During my second year at York University, I was enrolled in a project management course where I had to participate in developing a project with a team of my colleagues.

The focus on this project was to incorporate good project management techniques to a engineering project of our choosing, without actually executing the project. For our project, we chose to host a theoretical design competition for students around the university. My role in this project was to develop a website to handle advertisements and signup data for the competition.

Our project was well received, and our professor asked us to present it again on a later date in front of a panel of other engineering professors.
